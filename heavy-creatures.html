Heavy Creatures existed from 2004-2008. We made two LPs, toured a bit and played a ton of shows around New York City during that time. I started playing guitar at age 28, shortly after I moved here in 2001. I took lessons from Steve Ulrich of Big Lazy. Then in 2004, I started Heavy Creatures with Patrick and Sarah. We had a huge practice space in a basement in Dumbo. Chris joined in 2005 and we were totally complete. I absolutely loved being in a band, playing guitar, writing songs, playing live and making records. I was insanely driven. And as crazy and expensive as this city is, I think we are (and were then) lucky to have such an abundance of people here all passionate about their bands/projets/etc. It's so easy to get swept up in cynicism here because of all of this abundance. Overall, the creativity bounces around all of us and is quite positive.
