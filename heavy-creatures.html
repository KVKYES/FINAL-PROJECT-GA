Heavy Creatures was a band from 2004-2008. We made two LPs, toured a bit and played a bunch of shows around New York City. I started playing guitar at age 28, shortly after I moved here, in 2002. I took lessons from Steve Ulrich of Big Lazy. and then in 2004, started Heavy Creatures with Patrick and Sarah. We had a huge practice space in a basement in Dumbo. Chris joined in 2005 and we were totally complete. I absolutely loved being in a band, playing guitar, writing songs, playing live and making records. I was insanely driven. As crazy and expensive as this city is, I think we are (and were then) lucky to have such an abundance of people here all passionate about their bands. It's so easy to get swept up in cynacism but overall, the creativity bounces around all of us and is quite positive.
