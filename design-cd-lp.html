Winston Troy "Wild Bones"
EP Package (2010)

Winston Troy was my solo project for a year and a half between 2009-2010. The name was my great-geat aunt's name. I recorded one EP and toured a bit. For the packaging of the release, I could not afford vinyl. So, knowing that the music could easily be released thorugh Bandcamp or other digital means, I needed something physical to give away or sell. I got a bulk of CDs manuafactured but decided to do every bit of the packaging DIY.

I had quite a process: 1. First, I took random cardstock papers, watercolor paintings on paper, heavy-stock paper of any kind - cut, scored and folded to size. 2. I then sewed the sides of each piece of paper with random colors of thread to make a pocket of sorts. 3. Then, the insert (pictured here) was cut and folded to size to be inserted in. 4. The CD then went inside the insert, then went inside the paper pocket. 5. Drop the while thing into a Uline CD plasic envelope. 6. Next, the fun part. I inserted random cut thread, glitter, small cut paper and even actual shark teeth (found in Florida) inside the plasic envelope. I made sure these did not go inside the paper envelope with the CD.

Heavy Creatures "Loaded Sky"
CD Package (2006)

Heavy Creatures was a band that I started with Pat and Sarah in 2004. Chris joined in 2005 and we were complete. I did the illustrations and design of the entire package. We went the "normal" cd route by offset printing the inlay card and ordering plastic jewel cases. However, we did assemble the discs ourselves and inserted screenprinted art cards in random disc packages.
Illustrations were also painted by me, on white and cream acryclic on black mat board.

"Loaded Sky" front cover of cd package "Loaded Sky" back cover of cd package. Hand-painted lettering in an intentional "outsider art" (or "boiled P-nuts" roadside sign) style by me, also. "Loaded Sky" full insert for cd package. Screenprinted record release show poster along with art cards as promotional giveaways. Each card represents either the band "logo", signature horse image or a reference to a particular set of song lyrics on the cd. Also included are postcard-size versions of the poster to act as fliers to promote the release show.

Heavy Creatures
"The Cymbal and the Skull"
CD Package (2008)

These are from the second Heavy Creatures release. We decided to make a limited run of cds and packaged them in a two-part process. We chose to manufacture cds only, so we could have fun with the rest of the packaging ourselves. We printed the insert but did a rubber stamping process for the outer envelope.
For the envelope, we took individual random papers and scored & folded them. I had rubber stamps made of the front cover horse illustration for the front cover and the "disc" illustration for the back cover. We then used multiple colored stamp pads to create a layer effect. We had a ton of fun stamping the cover on each package!

Patrick Kauffman
"On The Mysterious Other"
CD package (2004)

This is my husband's solo record. It is beyond poetic. The design is straightforward yet has a long-ago feel to it. He has a passion for trains and wanted the cover to feel like an older folk record in its simplistic design. The cover photo is of a train from the New Jersey Southern Secondary line
