Heavy Creatures "Loaded Sky"
Record Release Poster (2006)

Heavy Creatures was a band that I started with Pat and Sarah in 2004. Chris joined in 2005 and we were complete. This is a screenprint record release show poster.

"Loaded Sky" screenprinted record release poster Screenprinted record release show poster along with art cards as promotional giveaways. Each card represents either the band "logo", signature horse image or a reference to a particular set of song lyrics on the cd. Also included are postcard-size versions of the poster to act as fliers to promote the release show. (Screenprinting by Kayrock)

Winston Troy show poster (2010)
This was used for various venues

Hand-sewn show poster that was able to be used and re-used. I was experiementing with the medium of the poster and learning to sew at the same time. (this is not the *best* sewing job in the world, but hey...it was fun!) Why not make a sewn poster for the venue and make color copies of it for the fliers? For each show, I would add an extra layer of fabric to the bottom area and embroider the lettering for the venues, date, time, etc. After each show, I could simply take the poster, remove the botttom fabric layer and and sew on a new bottom for another show. Of course, this only works with smaller venues, but those were the kind I was playing. It worked out great!
